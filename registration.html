Registration


The registration for AstroIAPS 2018 is open now! Fill up this form to register.


There are up to 46 available places.


Deadline for Payment

The participation fee is set at 90 € (= $110 USD = $2100 MXN), including accommodation and breakfast for the five days, transportation during the excursion, lunch at UNAM and all meals during the trip to the Observatory.

The optional trip to La Bufadora has an extra cost of €5 (= $6 USD = $110 MXN).

Following the registration period, we expect to email all applicants within 3 days. Payments will then be required (by PayPal) within 5 days of receiving an acceptance email from the organising committee.
