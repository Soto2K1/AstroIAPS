Programme


Here you can find the provisional programme for AstroIAPS five days excursion!

Besides all the UNAM facilites visits, lectures and workshops, the programme includes a Nations Evening where you can showcase some of your country's food and drink, a tour around some of Ensenada's most prominent landmarks, and an optional visit to La Bufadora.

Please do note that there are some details on Day 2 not included on the picture:

  • Workshop: Cosmology, will end at 14:30
  • Lunch will start at 14:30, and end at 16:15
  • Workshop: Astrobiology, will start at 16:15
